The slump chat awards 2025

Welcome!

In here, all messages of the past year were scanned, analysed and the results categorized in many interesting(?) categories.

To see how you did the past year, select your name at the top of the page after this tutorial concludes.

The different categories will be styled depending on how high you rank compared to others.

(You will also recieve Award-Points for your ranking, used for a special award!)

Click on an award to see everyone's analytics.

There are also certain awards for group effort.

(Everyone contributing to a "group effort" receives a Prism-tier award.)

About: Rankings and Points

Solo-Awards are tiered in 5 rankings:
Diamond #1 +7 pts.
Platinum #2 +5 pts.
Gold #3 - #4 +3 pts.
Silver #5 - #7 +2 pts.
Bronze #8 - #12 +1 pts.
Everyone contributing to a cooperative award will receive it's credit.
Prism Contributors +2 pts.
Awards with do not award points.
