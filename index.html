The slump chat awards 2025 - q1

Welcome!

In here, all messages of the past three months were scanned, analysed and the results categorized in many interesting(?) categories.

To see how you did, select your name at the top of the page after this tutorial concludes.

The different categories will be styled depending on how high you rank compared to others.

(You will also recieve Award-Points for your ranking, used for a special award!)

Click on an award to see everyone's scores on it.

About: Rankings and Points

Awards are tiered in 5 rankings:
Diamond #1 +7 pts.
Platinum #2 +5 pts.
Gold #3 - #4 +3 pts.
Silver #5 - #7 +2 pts.
Bronze #8 - #12 +1 pts.
Awards with do not award points.
